Lion- The King of Jungle

The lion is one of the five big cats in the genus Panthera and is a member of the family
Felidae.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Life span: 10- 14 years
  • Life Style / Diet: Carnivore
  • Inhabitat: Grasslands and Savannas

A lion’s roar can be heard from as far as 8 Km away. It heels don’t touch the ground when it walks.
Female lions are the main hunters.
